Rules

By reading these rules, you agree that you are over 18+. Also, agreeing to these rules means you are willing to follow Discord's Terms of Service and treat everyone with kindness and respect.

1. Follow Discord Terms and Guidelines

All members must adhere to Discord's official Terms of Service and Community Guidelines at all times.

2. Don't Post Illegal or Harmful Content

This includes rape jokes, child pornography, joking about consent, joking about minors, or any suicidal jokes. Such content is strictly prohibited.

3. No Outside and Relationship Dramas

This is not a dating server. Any drama related to outside relationships or personal conflicts will not be tolerated.

4. Don't DM Staff Regarding Server Related Issues

If you need to report something or someone, please use the command /report <your message>. You can also click here to open a support ticket.

5. No Disrespect or Harassment Towards Any Member

No body-shaming, name-calling, or harassment is allowed. Harassment towards members, including making anyone uncomfortable, disrespecting others, or persisting after being told to stop, is strictly prohibited. We prioritize a respectful and inclusive environment for all members.

6. No NSFW Outside of Marked NSFW Channels

NSFW content is only allowed in channels that are specifically marked as NSFW. Please respect this rule.

7. Don't Disrespect Staff

If staff tells you to drop an issue, just drop it. Respect their authority and the decisions they make.

8. No Racism or Hate Speech

Racism, hate speech, and discriminatory remarks of any kind will not be tolerated. Respect all members regardless of race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, etc.

9. Respect User's Roles, Pronouns, and Privacy

Please respect users' roles, pronouns, and any personal information they share. Do not use NSFW bot commands on people without their consent. If someone uses NSFW commands on you, please report it to staff by creating a ticket here.

10. Verification is Mandatory

If you want to actively communicate within this community, verification is mandatory for safety. To verify, please click here. You may post your own NSFW content only after verifying. If you leave the server, you will need to re-verify.

11. Any Form of Promotion is Strictly Prohibited

Promotion is strictly prohibited unless you're a VIP or VIP Plus member. If you're a VIP, you are allowed to have links in your bio, but these links must comply with Discord's Terms of Service and not break any rules. Server partners can also have their server link in their bio.

12. Alt Accounts are Strictly Prohibited

Alt accounts are not allowed in this server. If you see someone with an alt account, please report it to staff by creating a ticket here.

13. No Offensive Jokes

Offensive jokes or comments that target individuals or groups based on their appearance, background, or identity will not be tolerated. Maintain a friendly and respectful tone when engaging with others.
