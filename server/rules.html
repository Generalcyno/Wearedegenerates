Welcome to the We Are Degenerates NSFW 18+ Server

By reading these rules, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old and agree to abide by both these rules and Discord’s Terms of Service (ToS). Please treat everyone with kindness and respect.

General Rules

1. Adherence to Discord’s Terms of Service (ToS)

All activities on this server must comply with Discord’s Terms of Service and Community Guidelines. Familiarize yourself with these terms: here and here. Any violation can result in immediate and severe consequences, including a permanent ban.

2. Prohibition of Illegal or Harmful Content

  • No Illegal Content: Any content that violates local, national, or international laws is strictly prohibited.
  • No Harmful Content: Includes but is not limited to:
    • Sexual Violence Jokes: Jokes or content related to r*pe, sexual assault, or exploitation are strictly prohibited.
    • Child Pornography (CP): Any content involving minors in a sexual context will lead to an immediate ban.
    • Jokes About Consent: Any content or jokes that trivialize or mock the concept of consent are unacceptable.
  • Immediate Ban: Violation of this rule results in an immediate and permanent ban without prior warning.

3. No Outside Dramas

  • Personal Conflicts: Keep personal conflicts and disputes outside the server.
  • Drama-Free Zone: Engaging in drama or bringing external conflicts into the server is not allowed and may result in disciplinary action.

4. Reporting Issues Properly

  • Direct Messages: Do not contact staff members directly for server-related issues.
  • Reporting Commands: Use the /report <your message> command to report any issues or rule violations.
  • Support Ticket: For additional support or complex issues, click here to open a support ticket.

5. Respecting All Members

  • Personal Respect: Treat all members with courtesy and respect.
  • No Body Shaming: Avoid making derogatory comments about anyone’s appearance or body.
  • No Name-Calling: Refrain from using insulting or demeaning language towards others.

6. NSFW Content Restrictions

  • Designated Channels: NSFW content is only permitted in channels that are specifically labeled and designated for such content.
  • Content Outside NSFW Channels: Posting NSFW content outside of these channels will result in disciplinary action.

7. Zero Tolerance for Hate Speech

  • Racism and Discrimination: Racism, sexism, homophobia, or any form of hate speech is strictly prohibited.
  • Consequences: Such behavior will result in immediate removal from the server.

8. Re-Verification Policy

  • Leaving and Returning: If you leave the server and wish to return, you will need to go through the verification process again if you have been absent for more than 24 hours.
  • Re-Verification Link: To re-verify, click here and follow the provided instructions.

Breaking rules will result in a warning, kick, or ban depending on the staff team’s decision.
