Server Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2024/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to We are degenerates. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

Once a user completes ID verification, the bot may collect and store the following information:

  • User ID: A unique identifier assigned to your Discord account.
  • Username: Your publicly displayed Discord username.
  • User Account Creation Date: The date your Discord account was created.
  • Server Join Date: The date you joined the Discord server where the bot is used.
  • User Gender: If provided during ID verification.

This information is only collected for the server when verification is completed.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for the following purposes:

  • To verify your identity within the server.
  • To manage permissions and access to server features.
  • To ensure compliance with server rules and policies.
  • To provide a personalized experience based on server settings.

We do not sell, share, or distribute your data to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your data, including encryption and secure storage. Your information is retained only as long as necessary for the intended purpose and is deleted upon request or when you leave the server.

5. Data Sharing

Your data is not shared. Server administrators os staff members may have access to collected information for moderation purposes, but it will not be shared beyond the server environment.

6. User Control & Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data collected about you.
  • Request deletion of your data from our system.
  • Withdraw your consent to data collection by discontinuing use of the bot.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact the server administrators.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be communicated via the server where the bot is used. Continued use of the bot after updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us.

9. Disclaimer

We are not responsible for the leakage of any photos, content, or messages shared within the server. This is a public server, and users should be mindful of the content they share.

By joining We Are Degenerates, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
