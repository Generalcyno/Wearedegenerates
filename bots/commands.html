VIP guide

Welcome to VIP and VIP plus guide. Please note that the the VIP role section is for VIP plus only.
You will not get access to VIP category if you have not verified.

  • Setting up reacts
    in order to set up your reacts, your must create a ticket so a mod can set your reactions. You can create a ticket here.
  • VIP badges
    You can claim your VIP badge from here.
  • Private VC
    You can create a join to create VC by simply joining this channel.
    After that, the bot will create a new VC for you and move you there. once it moves you there, you will have access to edit the channel.
    To give someone access to join the VC, use /tempvc whitelist command. To remove access from a member, use /tempvc blacklist command.
  • Editing your VIP role (VIP Plus only)
    You can edit your VIP custom role any time.
    • Editing your role name:
      To edit your role name, use /vip-role command and choose the name option. Put whatever you like your role's name to be.
    • Editing your role color:
      To edit your role's color, use /vip-role command and choose the color option. Put whatever hex code you want your role color to be. You can find hex codes here.
    • Sharing your role:
      You can share your role with up to 4 more members. Use /vip-role and use the share option to add or remove someone from your custom role.
    • Editing your icon:
      To edit your icon, you can use multiple methods.
      • First method is to use an emoji from the server. You can use an emoji from we are degenerates server to edit your role icon by using /vip-role command and putting the emoji in icon option.
      • 2nd method is to use an image link. Simply enlarge your emoji with ,be command, click on the blue text and copy the link, use /vip-role command and put the link in icon option.
      • 3rd method is to use a message ID which has the image of icon you want by using /vip-role command and using icon option.
      • 4th method is to use an image. simply type ,vipicon but don't hit send. Click on attach file and attach the image (which you want as your icon) to your message and hit send. the bot will automatically change your icon.
